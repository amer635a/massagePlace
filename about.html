Things to Know
Although we do our best to meet your needs, we know that a spa could be a very personal experience. Please let the therapist know if there is any way she can improve your treatment or comfort. The spa opens 6 days a week, Mon to Sat, from 10 am to 10 pm. We encourage all guests to arrive 10 minutes prior to the scheduled appointment. Arriving without any rush will enhance the effect of your treatment. In case of running late, we will endeavor to conduct your full treatment. However, please note that the treatment may need to be shortened should there be another booking immediately afterwards. On your arrival to the spa, your therapist will conduct a brief consultation to ensure that you have the treatment best suited to your needs. Please advise the therapist if you are pregnant or have any health issue. An advance booking prior to your arrival is recommended to secure your preferred date and time treatment. Your privacy will be protected at all time. Your therapist will leave you to change in private and throughout the treatment you will be draped with towels, covering all parts of the body not being treated. To preserve the harmony withing the spa, kindly turn off your mobile phones.
